History of Porsche

Porsche, founded by Ferdinand Porsche in the 1930s, initially focused on engineering consulting before designing the Volkswagen Beetle. The introduction of the Porsche 356 and later the iconic 911 cemented Porsche's reputation for sports car excellence, blending performance and precision. Today, Porsche continues to innovate, offering a range of luxury vehicles that push the boundaries of automotive engineering.
